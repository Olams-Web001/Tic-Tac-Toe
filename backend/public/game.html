How to Play

  • Click on an empty square to make your move.
  • First to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!
  • If all squares are filled and no one wins, it's a draw.

Tip: You can play Versus (random online) or against the bot. The game mode is set on the home page.
